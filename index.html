Privacy Policy

Ride Akagali (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location Data: Used for ride tracking, navigation, and team ride features
  • Usage Data: Ride distance, speed, duration, and app interactions
  • Authentication: Anonymous login via Firebase for app functionality
  • Purchases: Subscription handled by Apple (we do not store payment info)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Track and display your rides
  • Enable team ride coordination
  • Improve app performance and features

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Your location is only shared with others when you participate in a team ride.

4. Data Storage

Data may be stored locally on your device or securely in cloud services such as Firebase.

5. Your Control

  • You can stop using the app to stop data collection

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13 years old.

7. Updates

This policy may be updated. Continued use of the app means you accept changes.
